# M4 safety boundary

The repository is currently limited to read-only evidence collection, deterministic paper evaluation, inert policy validation, and documentation.

The following capabilities are prohibited until separately reviewed and explicitly authorized:

- wallet connection
- ABI encoding and selector generation
- calldata and transaction-request construction
- state-changing simulation-provider calls
- signature or approval prompts
- transaction submission or retry logic
- receipt reconciliation
- live or tiny-live execution
- automated capital deployment

No current output authorizes implementation, simulation, signing, execution, or capital allocation.
